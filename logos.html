LOGOS

The PEACH logo was redesigned to highlight the analytical aspect of the platform. It is now monochromatic and elementary in appearance, to maximise flexibility of use.

The logo typeface is Montserrat, with a slightly wider letter spacing (tracking value of 50).

The PEACH logo should maintain its simplicity and spacing at all times. The following guidelines outline its proper use.
SIMPLE LOGO
The logo may be used on its own - a black logo for light backgrounds and a white logo for dark backgrounds. The images have been provided as SVG files. Please use SVG (rather than a raster file) wherever possible.
BOXED LOGO
The logo may also be used stylistically within a coloured box. In these cases, please use the guidance below to allow for the correct spacing within the box.
THEME-BASED LOGOS
Themes within PEACH may have their own variation of the logo, containing the name of the theme. In this section we outline the rules for spacing between the main logo and the theme name, as well as the spacing for a surrounding binding box (should you choose to use one).
DOWNLOADABLE LOGOS
In this section we provide SVGs of different logos (simple and theme-based). Please right-click on a logo to "Save Image As". These files may have long unwieldy filenames - please feel free to change the name.
We provide both a white logo and black logo.

Note that to download the white logo, please right-click on the text or bar-chart motif rather than the surrounding dark box.
