COLOURS

The PEACH platform incorporates varied applications, which will all undoubtedly require some degree of customisation. We leave the colour choices up to the different theme leads. However there are certain overarching guidelines and rules to ensure a degree of consistency and usability:
  • Please respect the guidelines in the components section.
    For example, certain elements should always be a certain colour (eg emergency notifications = red).
  • Consider using the Triad option on http://paletton.com/ to produce your colour scheme.
